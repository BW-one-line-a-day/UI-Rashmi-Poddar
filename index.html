An app for the 10 year journal

Portable & Secure Journal

This is the app version of the 10 year one line a day journal. Your daily entries for each day over 10 years are recorded and you are reminded each day to record a short and sweet fact from your day - a funny quote, a crazy milestone, or what you ate for dinner.

Fun Mood O Meter

You can add emojis or icons to your daily entries which signify how you were feeling that day.

Timeline

You can see what you were doing on a particular day for a number of years taking into account how long you have been using the app.
